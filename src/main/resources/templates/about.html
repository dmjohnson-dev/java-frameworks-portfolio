About Custom PC Workshop

Custom PC Workshop specializes in building and maintaining high-performance desktop PCs. We track our inventory of parts (CPUs, GPUs, RAM, storage, PSUs, cases) and assemble complete products tailored to customer needs.

What this app does

  • Manage parts and finished products
  • Edit, search, and maintain inventory levels
  • Prepare for purchasing and fulfillment workflows
